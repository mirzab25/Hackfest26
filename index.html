The shift

Most people care about the planet. What they don't have is a way to feel it. Green Groove turns invisible decisions into a landscape you can watch transform — every shorter shower, every meatless meal, every light switched off becomes a tree, a flower, a returning bird.

100 years from now

The world, in your hands.

Your daily habits aren't just points on a screen. They're a vote for which version of 2126 actually arrives. Scrub through four futures — each one's already on the table.

Cracked earth, dead trees, the skeletons of a world that took its environment for granted. This is where apathy leads.
